📌 Objective
The objective of this prompt pack is to design a reusable workflow that generates a complete SEO content cluster for a local business website.
Rather than generating isolated blog posts, the prompts follow a structured process that first plans the content strategy, then generates optimized articles, and finally organizes them into an interconnected content cluster. This approach reflects modern SEO practices used to build topical authority and improve local search visibility.

🧠 Prompt Engineering Approach
Instead of relying on a single prompt, the task was divided into multiple stages.
Each prompt builds upon the previous one, allowing the AI to generate more accurate, organized, and context-aware outputs.
The workflow consists of:
1.Content Planning
2.Pillar Blog Generation
3.Supporting Blog Generation
4.Internal Linking & Local SEO Validation
This modular structure makes the prompt system reusable for almost any local business.

🚀 Prompt Engineering Techniques Used
This project incorporates several prompt engineering techniques:
Role Prompting – Assigning the AI the role of an SEO strategist or content writer.
Context Injection – Providing business details, target audience, and location.
Constraint-based Prompting – Specifying word count, structure, headings, tone, and keyword usage.
Structured Output Formatting – Defining expected sections and formatting.
Iterative Prompt Refinement – Improving prompts after reviewing initial outputs.
Chain-of-Tasks Prompting – Breaking a large task into logical stages.

Step 1 — Content Cluster Planning
Objective
Plan the SEO strategy before generating content.
Prompt
Role:
You are an experienced SEO content strategist specializing in local business websites.

Business:
Smile Care Dental Clinic

Location:
Pune, Maharashtra, India

Task:
Create a content cluster consisting of:

• One comprehensive pillar topic
• Four supporting blog topics

The supporting topics should target different user intents, including:

• Cost-related queries
• Safety-related queries
• Decision-making queries
• Treatment-specific informational queries

For each topic provide:

• Blog title
• Primary search intent
• Brief explanation of why the topic supports the pillar article

Output in a clean table.
Why this prompt?
Content planning should come before content writing.
Starting with a topic cluster helps establish topical authority and ensures every supporting article has a clear purpose instead of generating unrelated blog posts.

Step 2 — Pillar Blog Generation
Objective
Generate a comprehensive authority article covering the primary topic.
Prompt
Role:
You are a professional SEO content writer specializing in healthcare websites.

Business:
Smile Care Dental Clinic

Location:
Pune, Maharashtra, India

Topic:
Best Dental Clinic in Pune – Services, Cost & What to Expect

Primary Keyword:
Best dental clinic in Pune

Requirements:

• 800–1000 words
• SEO-friendly H1 title
• Logical H2 and H3 hierarchy
• Engaging introduction
• Explain available services
• Discuss approximate treatment costs
• Describe what patients can expect during their first visit
• Explain why choosing a trusted local clinic matters
• Include an FAQ section
• Finish with a soft call-to-action

Constraints:

• Mention "Pune" naturally throughout the article.
• Avoid keyword stuffing.
• Maintain a trustworthy and patient-friendly tone.
• Write for humans before search engines.
Why this prompt?
My initial prompt produced content that lacked structure and depth.
Adding explicit requirements for headings, word count, tone, and section organization significantly improved readability and consistency.

Step 3 — Supporting Blog Generation
Objective
Generate supporting articles that target narrower search intent while strengthening the pillar page.
Prompt
Role:
You are an SEO content writer for a local dental clinic.

Business:
Smile Care Dental Clinic

Location:
Pune, Maharashtra, India

Generate four separate blog posts.

Topics:

1. Dental Implant Cost in Pune
2. Teeth Whitening – Is It Safe?
3. How to Choose the Right Dentist in Pune
4. Root Canal Treatment: What to Expect

Requirements for every article:

• 300–400 words
• SEO-friendly H1 title
• 2–3 H2 headings
• Short introduction using the primary keyword naturally
• Helpful and informative tone
• No duplicate content between articles
• End with a natural internal link pointing readers to the pillar article

Audience:
Patients looking for reliable information before scheduling a dental appointment.
Why this prompt?
Supporting blogs perform best when they answer one specific question instead of trying to cover everything.
Keeping the articles focused improves user experience while supporting the overall content cluster.

Step 4 — Internal Linking & Local SEO Validation
Objective
Verify that all generated content works together as a unified SEO strategy.
Prompt
Using the completed pillar blog and four supporting blogs:

1. Create an internal linking map.

2. Explain the search intent targeted by each article.

3. Confirm that:

• Local keywords appear naturally.
• Headings are SEO-friendly.
• Internal links strengthen topical authority.
• Keyword stuffing has been avoided.

Present the output as a table followed by a simple linking diagram.
Why this prompt?
Without internal linking, the blogs function as independent articles rather than a connected content cluster.
This step ensures the final output follows a logical SEO structure.

🔄 Prompt Refinement
During testing, several refinements improved the quality of the generated content.
Added explicit heading hierarchy.
Specified word count ranges.
Improved role definitions.
Added audience context.
Included formatting requirements.
Introduced internal linking constraints.
Reduced keyword repetition by emphasizing natural language.
Each refinement produced more structured and realistic SEO content.
